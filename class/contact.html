We have coursehelp@futuresystems.org to help students on course. Technical issues, course projects or web site will be supported.

Please contact additional instructors for help:

  • Badi' Abdul-Wahid
  • Hyungro Lee
  • Gregor von Laszewski (advanced concepts)
  • Fugang Wang (advanced concepts)

For problems with FutureSystems please email help@futuresystems.org. Be aware that the instructors are not recipients of emails sent to help@futuresystems.org so make sure to carbon copy then otherwise they will not be aware of your issue. Additionally, make sure that the instructors are carbon copied on your replies.

Important

Please be aware that questions addressed to staff should be sent between 9am and 4pm on business days. Questions outside those hours may not be addressed until the following business day. Please keep this in mind and start working on your assignments early.

Technical issues, course projects or web site will be supported.
