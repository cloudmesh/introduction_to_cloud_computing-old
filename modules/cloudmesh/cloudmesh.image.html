cloudmesh.image.cm_image module¶

Usage:
cm-image -h | –help cm-image version cm-image [–kind=KIND] info cm-image [–kind=KIND] [–gui] build OS cm-image [–kind=KIND] register OS
Arguments:
OS the OS you can find with cm-image list GUI yes or no
Options:
--gui switch on the gui. [default: False]
--kind=KIND the Kind of the image to be created. [default: vbox]
